   locking projector are explicit, but the repo has not yet reconstructed a

   unlabeled causal-set histories.

grading class, but applying the selected projector specifically to the weak
factor remains the causal-to-spinor physical bridge.  The repo's older claim
that Euclidean Hodge duality alone identifies a 2-form grading with physical
fermion gamma five has therefore been corrected: Hodge star supplies an
abstract grading, not a spin representation.
