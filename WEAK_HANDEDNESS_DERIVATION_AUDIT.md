## Result now proved

Dirac-spinor weak doublet with three independent binary indices:

- Dirac chirality;

On this space it proves the standard gamma-five involution, complementary and
orthogonal Weyl projectors, and the exact complexified weak-isospin relations

```text
[T3,T+] = T+,   [T3,T-] = -T-,   [T+,T-] = 2 T3.
```

The causal cylinder sign is connected to the fermion grading by the locking

```text

```

For the pure cylinder endpoints `Xi=+1` and `Xi=-1`, this is respectively the
standard left and right Weyl projector.  Within the affine algebra generated by

eigenspace and killing the relative `+1` eigenspace forces the coefficients
`1/2` and `-1/2`.

For every positive relational pseudoscalar and every projective refinement
depth, the induced charged-current vertex is nonzero, annihilates every right
Weyl state, and depends only on the left projection.  The reflected relational
datum gives the exact right-handed mirror.  Simultaneously reflecting the
causal orientation and the spinor preserves the law because both `Xi` and
`gammaFive` change sign.

This proves a precise relational statement:

> Every nonzero oriented causal branch carries exactly one nonzero chiral
> weak interaction, and that interaction is left-handed relative to the
> branch's causal orientation at every refinement depth.

With the convention in which the explicit positive cubic witness orients our
branch, the vertex is the standard left-handed charged current.

## Inputs that remain visible

The result is not yet an unconditional derivation of nature's vacuum from the
partial-order postulate alone.  Two physical bridges remain:

1. **Nonzero branch selection.** The current parity-even finite vacuum action
   cannot generate a preferred sign.  The machine-checked fixed-point no-go in
   `KFCausalSetChiralityGenerationNoGo.lean` proves this is impossible without
   a reflection-odd microscopic term or infinite-history spontaneous symmetry
   breaking.
2. **Continuum spin reconstruction.** The finite chirality/Weyl space and
   locking projector are explicit, but the repo has not yet reconstructed a
   Lorentzian spin bundle, continuum Dirac operator, and scaling limit from the
   unlabeled causal-set histories.

The locking formula itself is no longer arbitrary within the stated affine
grading class, but applying the selected projector specifically to the weak
factor remains the causal-to-spinor physical bridge.  The repo's older claim
that Euclidean Hodge duality alone identifies a 2-form grading with physical
fermion gamma five has therefore been corrected: Hodge star supplies an
abstract grading, not a spin representation.
