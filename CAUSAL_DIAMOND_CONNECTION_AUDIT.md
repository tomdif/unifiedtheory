# Causal diamond direction and connection-Laplacian audit

## Coordinate-free local directions

UnifiedTheory/Audit/KFCausalDiamondDirectionCover.lean defines an oriented
Hasse edge as a cover lower lessdot upper. Two edges are elementary-equivalent
when they are opposite sides of a commuting causal diamond; intrinsic local
directions are the equivalence classes generated by these moves.

This construction uses only the partial order. Every order isomorphism
preserves cover edges and diamonds, so it induces a direction equivalence.
Lean proves identity and composition laws for that transport.

On the elementary Boolean tangent cube Set (Fin 3), every cover edge inserts
a unique coordinate. Opposite-diamond moves preserve that coordinate, and
every edge is connected to the corresponding origin edge. Consequently the
diamond quotient is canonically equivalent to Fin 3 and has exactly three
classes.

This is stronger than assuming an abstract three-sheet set, but weaker than
requiring an exact local C_m^3 product atlas.

## Global-labeling obstruction

The same module defines directed path transport abstractly. A global
three-direction labeling forces transport to be path-independent. Therefore
two directed paths with the same endpoints and different induced transports
forbid a global labeling.

This theorem explains how a directed acyclic growth process can carry
holonomy: the relevant observable compares alternative paths, rather than
requiring a directed closed history.

It does not exhibit such a pair in the current causal/CSpec dynamics.

## Connection Laplacian

UnifiedTheory/Audit/KFCausalSheetConnectionLaplacian.lean equips a finite
state space with:

- positive stationary weights;
- a nonnegative row-stochastic transition law;
- detailed balance;
- reversible S3 sheet transport.

The sheet-twisted Markov operator and connection Laplacian are

    (T_W psi)(p) = sum_q P(p,q) U(p,q) psi(q),
    Delta_W = I - T_W.

Lean proves the exact energy identity

    <psi, Delta_W psi>_pi
      = (1/2) sum_pq pi(p) P(p,q)
          ||psi(p) - U(p,q) psi(q)||^2.

The quadratic form is nonnegative, and its zero set is exactly the parallel
sections on positive-weight edges. Equivalently, the kernel of Delta_W is
the parallel-section space.

## Full holonomy consequence

Positive paths compose their sheet permutations. If every S3 permutation
occurs as a closed-path transport at one base state, then a parallel section's
base value is fixed by all of S3. The earlier standard-carrier theorem forces
that value to vanish. If every state is positively reachable from the base,
the entire parallel section vanishes.

Hence full S3 holonomy plus positive connectivity implies:

    ker(Delta_W) = {0}

and every nonzero carrier field has strictly positive connection energy.
This is a qualitative finite spectral-gap statement; the module proves no
numerical lower bound and does not prove simplicity of the lowest eigenspace.

## Novelty and claim boundary

The individual ingredients in this package are mostly classical mathematics:
the standard representation of `S3`, regular-simplex vertices, directions of
a cube reconstructed from opposite square edges, Gram-kernel positivity, and
the connection-Laplacian energy identity all have established mathematical
ancestors. The finite rank-two witness is likewise elementary linear algebra
relative to the repo's scalar no-go.

The defensible novelty claim is narrower and architectural: the repository
composes these ingredients into a label-free, machine-checked chain from
Boolean causal order through intrinsic directions, sheet transport,
trace-zero carrier, centered birth current, twisted transfer, and
projectively consistent strongly positive histories. This audit does not
claim priority for any standalone ingredient, and it does not claim a new law
of nature.

A suitable current publication description is:

> A machine-checked synthesis deriving a canonical rank-two, strongly
> positive, projectively consistent history carrier from intrinsic
> three-direction Boolean causal geometry.

New theorem-level geometry would begin with deriving the direction cover or a
nontrivial monodromy witness from independently defined causal/CSpec data. New
physics would additionally require a dynamically selected state and an
observable consequence.

## Exact remaining boundaries

Three independent claims remain open:

1. **Local existence:** actual regular causal/CSpec neighborhoods have exactly
   three stable diamond-direction classes and assemble into a local cover.
2. **Global nontriviality:** one actual finite configuration has two
   same-endpoint paths with different direction transports, or equivalently a
   nontrivial direction cocycle.
3. **State selection:** the concrete causal connection has a distinguished
   lowest eigenline, or else its lowest-eigenspace projector is connected to
   the projective branch-amplitude construction.

The finite order geometry, transport functoriality, energy identity, and
full-holonomy kernel obstruction are proved. The three bullets above are not.
